When you sign in, you are agreeing to follow the terms of use outlined below and agree to let us use your personal data. We promise, this is a very short read.

TERMS OF USE

  1. The site is available to you as it is developed; it will contain errors and bugs. If you find one, please let us know.
  2. Do not break, hack, cause a denial of service or otherwise interfere with the normal operations of the site.
  3. You may (and are encouraged) to develop and extend the site, and use its source on your own server if you so choose. More details are on GitHub.
  4. You are responsible for any content you create.
  5. Follow the rules of the game.

PRIVACY

iFantasyFitness collects information such as your name, however we do not collect contact information like your email or phone number, nor do we see your password to any third-party service you sign in with. Here's exactly what information we collect and what we do with it, if you're a web nerd.

We also collect basic information about your computer, like what kind of browser you're using and whether you're on a Mac or a PC. This information is used for statistical purposes, so that we can continue to improve the site.

iFantasyFitness does not and will not have third-party advertisements. We don't believe in them or their silly tracking.

Your name and data about your fitness (how many points you have) is publicly available on the leaderboard.

iFantasyFitness does not sell your information for any reason, because that really shouldn't be happening.
